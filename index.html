Why Golden Retrievers Are the Best Dog Breed

There is already a debate between dog people and cat people about which are better, but within the dog people community, there is a debate about which breed of dog is the best. There are many different breeds of dog in the world. There are different colors, sizes, hair/fur types, etc. I have three different dogs that are different breeds and I love them all, but one breed(not dog) rises superior above all. Golden Retrievers. I have had three Golden Retrievers, one Golden Goodle, and two other breed of dogs.

DISCLAIMER: I love all of my dogs equally. This is simply about which breed is the best overall. Thank you to Holly, Casco, Ocho, Coco, Fanta, and Domino for helping in the research of this project.

Here is why Golden Retrievers are the best dog breed:

  • Cuddles/Emotional Support
  • Mixes With Other Doggos
  • Loyalty
